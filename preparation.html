🕵️
Activity 1 — Guess the Word!
Read the description. Write the word on the line. Click 👁️ to check!
Clue 1
"She is a family member. She is your mum's or dad's mum. She is usually older. She loves her grandchildren."
→ grandmother
Clue 2
"He lives underground. He is always late. He wears a coat and has a big pocket watch. He is white and has long ears."
→ White Rabbit
Clue 3
"You feel this emotion when your friend has something you don't. You want what they have. It is a negative feeling."
→ jealousy
Clue 4
"He wears a very big, strange hat. He always has a tea party. He is a bit crazy and very funny. He is a character in Alice in Wonderland."
→ Mad Hatter
Clue 5
"This is an activity. You do it in a pool or the sea. You use your arms and legs. It is great exercise and you get wet!"
→ swimming
Clue 6
"She is a very angry Queen. She shouts 'Off with their heads!' a lot. She plays a strange sport with flamingos."
→ Red Queen
Clue 7
"You feel this when you do something wrong and everyone is watching you. Your face goes red. You want to hide!"
→ embarrassment
Clue 8
"He is your parent's brother. He is not your dad, but he is family. His children are your cousins."
→ uncle

Activity 2 — Which Description is Correct?
Read the description. Circle or click the right answer.
1. "It is a hobby. You do it outside. You use a bicycle. You go fast on roads or in the countryside."
2. "She is a family member. She is your father's sister. She is not your mother."
3. "You feel this emotion when you cannot wait. Something exciting is going to happen. You feel lots of energy and happiness."

5. "He is a cat in Alice in Wonderland. He can disappear and only his smile is left. He is very mysterious."
6. "You do this hobby with wool and two long needles. You make jumpers, socks and scarves."

Year 2 — Can you describe it?

Food · Clothes · Haitian Revolution · Romeo and Juliet
🕵️
Activity 1 — Guess the Word!
Read the description. Write the word on the line. Click 👁️ to check!
Clue 1
"It is a food. It is Italian. It is round and flat. You put cheese, tomato and toppings on it. You cook it in a very hot oven."
→ pizza
Clue 2
"She is a character in a Shakespeare play. She is very young. She falls in love with someone from an enemy family. In the end, she dies."
→ Juliet
Clue 3
"It is a piece of clothing. You wear it when it rains. It keeps you dry. It often has a hood. It is waterproof."
→ raincoat
Clue 4
"He was a real person from Haiti. He was born into slavery but became a great leader. He fought for freedom and independence against the French."
→ Toussaint Louverture
Clue 5
"It is a food from Japan. It has rice and often raw fish. You eat it with chopsticks or your hands. It is very popular worldwide."
→ sushi
Clue 6
"He is a religious man in Romeo and Juliet. He helps Romeo and Juliet get married in secret. He makes a plan but it goes very wrong."
→ Friar Lawrence
Clue 7
"It is a piece of casual clothing. It is very comfortable. It has a hood. Young people love to wear it. It is usually made of soft material."
→ hoodie
Clue 8
"This is a big historical event. In 1791, enslaved people in Haiti rose up against their French masters. They fought for their freedom."
→ revolt / rebellion

Activity 2 — Which Description is Correct?
Read the description. Circle or click the right answer.
1. "It is a French food. You eat it for breakfast. It is buttery, golden and crescent-shaped."
2. "You wear these on your feet in summer. They are open and flat. You wear them at the beach."
3. "This is what Romeo and Juliet's two families do. They hate each other and they fight constantly."
4. "In Haiti, enslaved people worked on these large farms. They grew sugar cane for the French."
5. "This piece of clothing is very formal and elegant. Men wear it at weddings and important events. It is usually black and white."
6. "Romeo and Juliet meet for the first time at this event. Everyone wears a costume and hides their face."

Year 3 — Can you describe it?

Jobs · 1984 / Orwell · Macbeth · Music · Linguistic Relativity
🕵️
Activity 1 — Guess the Word!
Read the description. Write the word on the line. Click 👁️ to check!
Clue 1
"This person works in a hospital. They do operations to save people's lives. They need many years of study. They wear a mask in the operating room."
→ surgeon
Clue 2
"This is a famous novel by George Orwell. It is set in the future. Someone called Big Brother watches everyone. The people have no freedom."
→ 1984
Clue 3
"This instrument has six strings. You can play rock, blues or pop with it. Jimi Hendrix and Kurt Cobain were very famous for playing it."
→ guitar
Clue 4
"This animal appears in Animal Farm. It is pink. It makes a loud oink sound. Napoleon and Snowball are both this type of animal."
→ pig
Clue 5
"She appears in Macbeth. She has magic powers. She makes dark predictions about the future. She is mysterious and dangerous."
→ witch
Clue 6
"This person makes food in a restaurant or hotel. They wear a white uniform and sometimes a tall white hat. Gordon Ramsay is a very famous one."
→ chef
Clue 7
"This is a language in Orwell's 1984. The government invented it. It has fewer and fewer words every year. The goal is to make independent thought impossible."
→ Newspeak
Clue 8
"This person flies aeroplanes. They carry passengers from one country to another. They wear a uniform and a hat. They speak to the control tower by radio."
→ pilot
🎯
Activity 2 — Which Description is Correct?
Read the description. Circle or click the right answer.
1. "This person fixes the water pipes in your house when they are broken."
2. "In 1984, this is the secret police. They arrest people for having bad thoughts about the government."
3. "You play this instrument by hitting it. It keeps the rhythm. Rock and jazz bands always have one."
4. "In Animal Farm, this animal is very strong and works very hard. He always says 'I will work harder.' He represents the working class."
5. "In Macbeth, this is what Macbeth sees floating in front of him before he kills the king. It is not real."
6. "This person writes music and the words to songs. Taylor Swift and Ed Sheeran are famous examples."
